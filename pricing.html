Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and features

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a trial period?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan, no credit card required. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time during or after the trial.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team for more information.

Can I pay annually?

Yes, annual billing is available for all paid plans and comes with a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.
