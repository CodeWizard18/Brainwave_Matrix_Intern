Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I request a demo?

You can request a personalized demo by filling out the contact form above or by emailing sales@tasknest.com with your name, company, and team size.

What support options are available?

We offer email support for all plans, with Pro users receiving priority email support. Enterprise customers enjoy 24/7 dedicated support via email, chat, and phone.

Can I import data from another tool?

Yes, TaskNest supports importing data from popular tools like Trello, Asana, and Jira. Our team can help facilitate larger migrations for Enterprise customers.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, TaskNest is available on iOS and Android devices. You can download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store to access your tasks on the go.
